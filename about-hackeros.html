About HackerOS

HackerOS is a Debian-based operating system designed for security enthusiasts, developers, and professionals. It provides a robust platform with pre-installed tools for cybersecurity, development, and system administration.

Our mission is to create a powerful and user-friendly environment for exploring the world of technology and security.
